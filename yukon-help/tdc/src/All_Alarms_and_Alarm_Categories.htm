All Alarms and Alarm Categories

The All Alarms display shows all of the current alarms on the Yukon system.

 

Alarm Category 1 through Category 31 show the alarms on the Yukon system for the selected category.

 

To acknowledge an alarm, right-click on an alarm, select the Ack Alarm option, and click on the alarm that appears. When an alarm is acknowledged, the alarm stops flashing and the audible alarm stops. However, the alarm still displays in the table until the point that caused the alarm changes to a non-alarming state or value.
