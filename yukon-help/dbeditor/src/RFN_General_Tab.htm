RFN General Tab

The following changes can be made to the general settings of the RFN:

 

  1. The name of the RFN can be changed in the Device Name text box.

  2. The serial number of the RFN can be changed in the Serial Number text box.

  3. Do not change the name of the manufacturer, unless it was entered incorrectly when the RFN was created. The name of the manufacturer must be entered in the Manufacturer text box exactly as shown in the following table:

  1. The RFN can be disabled or enabled by selecting or clearing the Disable Device check box. Select this check box to take the RFN out of service. Clear this check box to enable the RFN.
