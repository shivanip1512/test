Two-Way LCR Scan Rate Tab

The following changes can be made to the scan rate settings of a two-way LCR:

 

  1. The Demand & Status Rate scan category can be enabled or disabled by selecting or clearing the category Demand & Status Rate check box.

  2. If the check box of the Demand & Status Rate scan category is selected, the following changes can be made to the scan settings:

    1. A different default scan interval can be selected from the Interval drop-down list.

    2. A different alternate scan interval can be selected from the Alt Interval drop-down list.

    3. A different group can be selected from the Group drop-down list. The scan group is used to assign priorities to the scan category.
