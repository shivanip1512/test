Devices

A device is defined as the physical field hardware used by the Master Station software to gather data.

 

This includes power line carrier infrastructure hardware, data gathering devices (using the communications mediums of carrier, phone, and radio), and one-way control devices.

 

The following devices can be created in the Yukon Editor.

  • Metering and Control Devices (MCT)

  • Signal Transmitters

  • Electronic Meters

  • Remote Terminal Units (RTU)

  • Virtual Devices
