Energy Exchange

Energy Exchange provides customers with the opportunity to shed load in exchange for energy credits.

 

If you selected Energy Exchange:

 

  1. Enter a heading for the message in the Heading text box. This is used as the subject in e-mails.

  2. Enter text in the Header text box. This text is placed at the beginning each notification message.

  3. Enter text in Footer text box. This is the text placed at the end of each notification message.

  4. Enter text in the Canceled text box. This is the message that is sent to the customer if the curtailment is cancelled.

  5. Enter text in the Stopped Early text box. This is the message that is sent to the customer if the curtailment offer is stopped early.

  6. Set the minimum notification time in the Min Notify Time box. This is the minimum number of minutes before the start of the curtailment period to send out the notification message.

  7. Click Next.

  8. Assign customers to or unassign customers from the energy exchange program.

    • To assign a customer, select the customer from the Available Customers list and click Add. To select more than one customer, hold Ctrl key on the keyboard while clicking additional customers.

    • To unassign a customer, select the customer from the Assigned Customers list and click Remove. To select more than one customer, hold Ctrl key on the keyboard while clicking additional customers.

  9. Click Finish. The new load program appears in the Program Editor. For instructions on editing a load program, refer to the related topics.
