MCT Settings Tab

A disconnect device can be assigned to an MCT-410 on this tab:

 

  1. To enable communication with the disconnect meter base on an MCT-410, select the Enable Disconnect check box.
    To disable communication with the disconnect meter base, clear the Enable Disconnect check box.

  2. If the Enabled Disconnect check box is selected, enter the physical address of the MCT-410D disconnect meter base in the Disconnect Address text box.
