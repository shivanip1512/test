Macro Route

If Macro Route was selected as the route type:

 

  1. Enter a name for the macro route in the Route Macro Name text box.

  2. Click the Next button.

  3. To assign communication routes to the Macro Route, select the communication route from the Available list and click the Add button. To select more than one communication route, hold the Ctrl key on the keyboard while clicking additional communication routes.

Note: The same communication route may be assigned to the macro route more than once and communication routes are taken in order from top to bottom in the Assigned list. To rearrange the order of the assigned communication routes, click and drag on a communication route to move it up or down the Assigned list.

To unassign a route, select the route from the Assigned list and click the Remove button. To select more than one route, hold the Ctrl key on the keyboard while clicking additional routes.

  1. Click the Finish button. The new macro route appears in the Route Editor.
