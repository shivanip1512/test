Editing an Energy Exchange Program

The following changes can be made to a energy exchange program:

  1. Click the Energy Exchange tab. The Energy Exchange tabbed page appears.

    • The heading of the message can be changed in the Heading text box. This text is used as the subject in e-mails.

    • The header and footer text can be changed in the Header and Footer text boxes. This is the text placed at the beginning and end of each notification message.

    • The cancelled and stopped early text can be changed in the Canceled and Stopped Early text boxes. This is the message that is sent to the customer if the curtailment is cancelled or stopped early.

    • The minimum notification time can be changed in the Min Notify Time spin box. This is the minimum number of minutes before the start of the curtailment period to send out the notification message.

  2. Click the Exchange Customers tab. The Energy Customers tabbed page appears. You may assign customers to or unassign customers from the load program.

    • To assign a customer, select the customer from the Available list and click Add. To select more than one customer, hold Ctrl key on the keyboard while clicking additional customers.

    • To unassign a customer, select the customer from the Assigned list and click Remove. To select more than one customer, hold the Ctrl key on the keyboard while clicking additional customers.
