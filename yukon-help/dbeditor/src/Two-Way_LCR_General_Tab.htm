Two-Way LCR General Tab

The following changes can be made to the general settings of the two-way LCR:

 

  1. The name of the LCR can be changed in the Device Name text box.

  2. The serial number can be changed in the Serial Number text box. The factory-supplied serial number is located on a label that is affixed to the LCR board inside the LCR enclosure.

  3. The LCR can be disabled or enabled by selecting or clearing the Disable Device check box. Select this check box to take the LCR out of service, which suspends scanning and alarming. Clear this check box to enable the LCR.

  4. A different route can be selected from the Communication Route drop-down list.
