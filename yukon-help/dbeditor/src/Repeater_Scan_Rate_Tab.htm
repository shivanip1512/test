Repeater Scan Rate Tab

The following changes can be made to the scan rate settings of a repeater:

 

  1. The periodic health check scan category can be enabled or disabled by selecting or clearing the Periodic Health Check check box. The periodic health check scan periodically checks that the repeater is functioning.

 

When the check box of the periodic health check scan category is selected, the following changes can be made to the scan settings:

    1. A different default scan interval can be selected from the Interval drop-down list.

    2. A different alternate scan interval can be selected from the Alt Interval drop-down list.

    3. A different group can be selected from the Group drop-down list. The scan group is used to assign priorities to the scan category.

  1. The optional scan window can be enabled or disable by selecting or clearing the Enabled check box. The Optional Scan Window defines a time when the scans can occur.

    1. A different scan type can be selected from the Type drop-down list.

    2. The time at which the scan window starts can be changed in the Open text box.

    3. The time at which the scan window stops can be changed in the Close text box.
