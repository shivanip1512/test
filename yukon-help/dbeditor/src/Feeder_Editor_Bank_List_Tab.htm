Feeder Editor Bank List Tab

The order, from top to bottom, is the order in which banks are switched. To reorder, drag a cap bank up or down in the list.

  • To add a cap bank, select the bank from the Banks Available list and click Add. To select more than one bank, hold the Ctrl key on the keyboard while clicking additional banks.

  • To remove a cap bank, select the bank from the Banks Assigned list and click Remove. To select more than one bank, hold the Ctrl key on the keyboard while clicking additional banks.
