Editing a Season

To edit a load control season:

 

  1. Select the season to edit from the Assigned Seasons list.

  2. Click Edit.

  3. The name of the season can be changed in the Season Name text box.

  4. Under Start Date, a different month and day can be selected from the Month and Day drop-down lists as the start of the load control season.

  5. Under End Date, a different month and day can be selected from the Month and Day drop-down lists as the end of the load control season.

  6. Click OK to save the changes.
