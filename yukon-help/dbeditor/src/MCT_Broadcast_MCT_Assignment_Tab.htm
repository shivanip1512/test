MCT Broadcast MCT Assignment Tab

The following changes can be made to the MCT assignment settings of an MCT Broadcast group:

  • To assign an MCT to the MCT Broadcast group, select an MCT from the Available MCTs list and click the Add button. To select more than one MCT, hold the Ctrl key on the keyboard while clicking additional MCTs.

  • To rearrange the order of the assigned MCTs, click and drag an MCT to move it up or down the list.

  • To unassign an MCT from the MCT Broadcast group, select an MCT from the Assigned MCTs list and click the Remove button. To select more than one MCT, hold the Ctrl key on the keyboard while clicking additional MCTs.
