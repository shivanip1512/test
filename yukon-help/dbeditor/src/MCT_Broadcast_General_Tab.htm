MCT Broadcast General Tab

The following changes can be made to the general settings of an MCT Broadcast group:

  1. The name of the MCT Broadcast group can be changed in the Device Name text box.

  2. The lead meter physical address of the MCT Broadcast group can be changed in the Lead Address text box. The lead meter address is shared among all of the MCTs that the utility wants to group together.

  3. The MCT Broadcast group can be disabled or enabled by selecting or clearing the Disable Device check box. Select this check box to disable the MCT Broadcast group. Clear this check box to enable the MCT Broadcast group.

  4. A different communication route can be selected from the Communication Route drop-down list.
