Creating an MCT Broadcast Group

Create an MCT Broadcast group after creating MCTs.

 

Perform the following steps to create an MCT broadcast group:

 

  1. From the View menu, select Core.

  2. From the Create menu, select Device.

  3. Select MCT from the list on the left.

  4. Select MCT Broadcast from the list on the right.

  5. Click the Next button.

  6. Enter a name for the MCT Broadcast group in the Device Name text box.

  7. Enter the lead meter address of the MCT Broadcast group in the Lead Meter Address text box. The lead meter address is shared among all of the MCTs that the utility wants to group together.

  8. Click the Next button.

  9. Assign MCTs to the MCT Broadcast group.

    • To assign an MCT to the MCT Broadcast group, select an MCT from the Available list and click the Add button.
      To select more than one MCT, hold the Ctrl key on the keyboard while clicking additional MCTs.

    • To rearrange the order of the assigned MCTs, click and drag an MCT to move it up or down.

    • To unassign an MCT from the MCT Broadcast group, select an MCT from the Assigned list and click the Remove button.

  10. Click the Next button.

  11. Select the communication route for the MCT Broadcast group from the Select the route used with this device drop-down list.

  12. Click the Finish button. The new MCT Broadcast group is displayed in the Device Editor. For instructions on editing an MCT Broadcast group, refer to the related topics.
