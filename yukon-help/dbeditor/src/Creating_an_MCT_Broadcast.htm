Creating an MCT Broadcast

Create an MCT Broadcast group after you have finished creating MCT devices.

 

If you selected MCT Broadcast as the device type:

 

  1. Enter a name for the MCT Broadcast group in the Device Name text box.

  2. Enter the lead meter’s physical address in the Lead Meter Address text box. The lead meter address is shared among all MCTs which a utility wants to group together.

  3. Click the Next button.

  4. You may assign MCTs to or remove MCTs from the MCT Broadcast group.

    • To assign an MCT, select the MCT from the Available list and click the Add button. To select more than one MCT, hold the Ctrl key on the keyboard while clicking additional MCTs.

    • To rearrange the order of the assigned MCTs, click and drag on an MCT to move it up or down.

    • To unassign an MCT, select the MCT from the Assigned list and click the Remove button. To select more than one MCT, hold the Ctrl key on the keyboard while clicking additional MCTs.

  5. Click the Next button.

  6. Select the communication route for the MCT Broadcast group from the drop-down list of routes.

  7. Click the Finish button. The new MCT Broadcast group appears in the Device Editor. For instructions on editing a device, refer to the related topics.
