Creating a Contact

Perform the following steps to create a contact:

 

  1. From the View menu, select System.

  2. From the Create menu, select Contact.

  3. Enter the first name of the contact in the First Name text box.

  4. Enter the last name of the contact in the Last Name text box.

  5. To assign a Yukon login account to the contact, select the login from the Yukon Login drop-down list.

  6. Select the type of notification from the Notification Type drop-down list. The notification types are:

    • Email

    • Phone Number

    • Email to Pager

    • Fax Number

    • Home Phone

    • Work Phone

    • Voice PIN

    • Cell Phone

    • Email to Cell

    • Call Back Phone

    • IVR Login

    1. Depending on the notification type selected in step 6, enter either the e-mail address, phone number, or pin/login of the contact into the Address text box.

    2. To prevent the e-mail address or phone number from being used for notification purposes, select the Disable Usage check box.

    3. Click the Add button. The notification information is added to the list at the bottom of the Contact Editor.
      To remove notification information from the contact, select the notification information to remove from the list and click the Remove button.

  7. Click the Next button.

  8. Enter the street address of the contact in the Address text boxes.

  9. Enter the city in which the contact is located in the City text box.

  10. Enter the county in which the contact is located in the County text box.

  11. Select the state in which the contact is located from the State drop-down list.

  12. Enter the zip code of the contact in the Zip text box.

  13. Click the Finish button. The new contact appears in the Contact Editor. For instructions on editing a contact, refer to the related topics.
