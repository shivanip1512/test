MCT Scan Rate Tab

The following changes can be made to the scan rate settings of the device:

 

  1. The scan categories can be changed by selecting or clearing the category check boxes. The scan categories that are displayed depend on the type of MCT:

 

If the MCT is an MCT-410cL, MCT-410fL, or MCT-410iL, the following scan categories are displayed:

    • Demand & Voltage Rate – Collects demand and voltage point values.

    • Accumulator (Energy) Rate – Collects kWh point values.

 

If the MCT is not an MCT-410cL, MCT-410fL, or MCT-410iL, the following scan categories are displayed:

    • Demand & Status Rate – Collects demand and status point values.

    • Accumulator (Energy) Rate – Collects kWh point values.

  1. When the check box of a scan category is selected, the following scan settings can be changed:

    1. A different default scan interval can be selected from the Interval drop-down list.

    2. A different alternate scan interval can be selected from the Alt Interval drop-down list.

    3. A different group can be selected from the Group drop-down list. The scan group is used to assign priorities to the scan category.

  2. The optional scan window can be enabled or disable by selecting or clearing the Enabled check box. The Optional Scan Window defines a time when the scans can occur.

    1. A different scan type can be selected from the Type drop-down list.

    2. The time at which the scan window starts can be changed in the Open text box.

    3. The time at which the scan window stops can be changed in the Close text box.
