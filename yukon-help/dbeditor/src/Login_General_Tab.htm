Login General Tab

The following changes can be made to the general settings of the login account:

  1. Enable or disable the login account by selecting or clearing the Login Enabled check box.

  2. The username can be changed in the User Name text box.

  3. To change the password, enter a new password in the Password and Retype Password text boxes.

  4. If the Link to Energy Company check box is selected, the energy company that the login account is assigned to can be changed by selecting a different energy company from the drop-down list.

  5. To unassign an energy company from the login account, clear the Link to Energy Company check box.
