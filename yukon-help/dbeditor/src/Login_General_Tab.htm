Login General Tab

The following changes can be made to the general settings of a login account:

  1. Enable or disable the login account by selecting or clearing the Login Enabled check box.

  2. The username can be changed in the User Name text box.

  3. The authentication type can be changed by selecting a different type from the drop-down list:

    • Normal - The password is stored as plain-text in the Yukon database.
      The normal authentication type is required for Yukon STARS login accounts.

    • Hashed - The password is converted to a sequence of letters and numbers and is then stored in the Yukon database.

    • Radius - A radius login server is used to authenticate passwords.

    • No Login - The password is disabled and the user cannot log in.

  4. If either Normal or Hashed is selected as the authentication type, the password can be changed by clicking the Change Password button.

  5. If the Link to Energy Company check box is selected, the energy company that the login account is assigned to can be changed by selecting a different energy company from the drop-down list.

  6. To unassign an energy company from the login account, clear the Link to Energy Company check box.
