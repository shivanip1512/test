Creating a Season

To add a load control season to the schedule:

 

  1. Click the Create button. The Season Date Creation dialog box appears.

  2. Enter a name for the season in the Season Name text box.

  3. Under Start Date, select the month and day that is the start of a load control season from the Month and Day drop-down lists.

  4. Under End Date, select the month and day that is the end of a load control season from the Month and Day drop-down lists.

  5. Click the OK button.
