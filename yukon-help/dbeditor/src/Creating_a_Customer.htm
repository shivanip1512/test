Creating a Customer

To create a customer:

 

  1. From the View menu, select System.

  2. From the Create menu, select Customer.

  3. Enter the name of the company in the Company Name text box.

  4. (Optional) Set the home directory in the Home Directory text box. This is the folder in which customer information is stored.

  5. (Optional) Select the customer's energy company from the Owner Energy Company drop-down list.

  6. Select the primary customer contact from the Primary Contact drop-down list.

  7. If the primary contact does not exist, click the New Contact button to add a contact.

    1. Enter the first name of the contact in the First Name text box.

    2. Enter the last name of the contact in the Last Name text box.

    3. Enter the email address of the contact in the Email Address text box.

    4. Click OK to save the new contact.

  8. Select the time zone of the customer from the Time Zone drop-down list.

  9. (Optional) Enter a tracking number for the customer in the Customer Number text box.

  10. Click Next.

  11. Enter the address in the Address text boxes.

  12. Enter the city in the City text box.

  13. Enter the county in the County text box.

  14. Select the state from the State drop-down list.

  15. Enter the zip code in the Zip text box.

  16. Click Next.

  17. To assign an additional contact, select the contact from the Available Additional Contacts drop-down list and click Add.

    To unassign an additional contact, select the contact from the Assigned Contacts list and click Remove.

  18. Click Finish. The new customer appears in the Customer Editor. For instructions on editing a customer, refer to the related topics.
