Creating a Customer

Perform the following steps to create a customer:

 

  1. From the View menu, select System.

  2. From the Create menu, select Customer.

  3. Enter the name of the company in the Company Name text box.

  4. To specify a home directory, enter the directory path in the Home Directory text box. This is the directory in which customer information is stored.

  5. To specify the owner energy company of the customer, select the name of the company from the Owner Energy Company drop-down list.

  6. Select the primary customer contact of the customer from the Primary Contact drop-down list.

  7. If the primary contact does not exist, click the New Contact button to add a contact.

    1. Enter the first name of the contact in the First Name text box.

    2. Enter the last name of the contact in the Last Name text box.

    3. Enter the e-mail address of the contact in the E-mail Address text box.

    4. Click the OK button to save the new contact.

  8. Select the time zone of the customer from the Time Zone drop-down list.

  9. To enter a tracking number for the customer, enter the tracking number in the Customer Number text box.

  10. Click the Next button.

  11. Enter the street address of the customer in the Address text boxes.

  12. Enter the city in which the customer is located in the City text box.

  13. Enter the county in which the customer is located in the County text box.

  14. Select the state in which the customer is located from the State drop-down list.

  15. Enter the zip code of the customer in the Zip text box.

  16. Click the Next button.

  17. To assign an additional contact to the customer, select the contact from the Available Additional Contacts drop-down list and click the Add button.

To unassign an additional contact to the customer, select the contact from the Assigned Contacts list and click the Remove button.

  1. Click the Finish button. The new customer appears in the Customer Editor. For instructions on editing a customer, refer to the related topics.
