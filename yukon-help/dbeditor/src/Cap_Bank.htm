Cap Bank

Cap Bank databases entries represent individual field capacitor banks. Information about banks includes bank size in kVAr, location, status point, and control point. The control point can be any controllable status point in the Yukon database. This could be a point off an RTU, however, will most likely be a point from a CBC.

 

A bank without a control point setting represents a fixed Cap Bank. An additional operations counter point is supported which is incremented by one for every open or closed operation.
