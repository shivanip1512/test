Get Data Now

To perform a read using the alternate scanning rate of a device:

  1. Select a trend with a data point from a device that has an alternate scanning rate.

  2. Select Get Data Now from the Trend menu.
    Note: The read may take up to 30 minutes to complete, depending on the amount of data that is being retrieved.

  3. After enough time has passed for the read to complete, click the Refresh toolbar button to refresh the trend with the data collected during the read, which is stored in the Yukon database.
