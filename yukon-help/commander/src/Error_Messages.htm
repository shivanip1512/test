Error Messages

The communications error messages are categorized in two groups:

  • Power Line Communication Errors – Hardware errors that occurred at or beyond the point of injection on the distribution primary. These errors are not relevant to radio load management systems.

  • Bulk Communications Errors – Hardware errors that occurred in communications between the master station and remote devices. These may be related to the phone line or data radio which links the master station to the remote devices.
