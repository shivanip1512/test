Error 20: Word 1 NACK Padded

Description

No return message received at the CCU.

 

Probable Reason

Failed two-way device or inadequate communication throughput.

 

Solution

First, check to verify that the two-way device is functional. If the two-way device is functional, any of the following may correct the problem:

  • Check input portion of CCU (Receiver card).

  • Check output portion of CCU (power amp, driver cards, P-card, SH card).

  • Check SSSA (Solid State Surge Arrestor) in SCU.

  • Check gas tube arrestor in CCU.

  • Check CCU power supply module.

  • Retune the PCA coupled to the CCU.

  • Add an RPT/PCA onto the distribution line.

  • Add an SCU-830 enhancer (H-field coupler) or ensure existing unit is operational and not failed.

  • Check CCU back plane.

  • Check coax cable and N-Type connector.
